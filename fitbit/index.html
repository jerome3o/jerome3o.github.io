Family Fitness - Product Description

Family Fitness is a web app that allows you to track your fitness progress alongside that of your family members. By connecting your Fitbit device, you can see a visual representation of your steps, distance traveled, and other fitness metrics. Our app also includes features such as goal tracking and progress monitoring to help you stay motivated and achieve your fitness goals. With Family Fitness, you can stay connected with your loved ones and encourage each other to lead a healthy, active lifestyle.
