Family Fitness - Privacy Policy

At Family Fitness, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our web app.

  2. Use of Information: We use the information we collect from you to provide you with a visual representation of your fitness progress compared to that of your family members. We will not share your data with any third parties.
  3. Security of Information: We take the security of your information seriously and have implemented appropriate measures to protect it. However, we cannot guarantee the complete security of your information and you acknowledge that there may be some risk of unauthorized access or use.
  4. Changes to This Privacy Policy: We reserve the right to update and modify this privacy policy at any time. We will notify you of any significant changes and give you the opportunity to review and accept the revised policy.
  5. Contact Us: If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact me at jeromeswannack@gmail.com.

By using Family Fitness, you acknowledge that you have read and understand this privacy policy and agree to be bound by it. If you do not agree to this privacy policy, please do not use our web app.
